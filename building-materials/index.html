Star Rose Group of Company

Star Rose Building Materials Trading is one of the leading and pioneer Building Materials, Safety & Industrial Equipment's suppliers in the United Arab Emirates. Our business partners and support companies are present in China, Europe, GCC, Germany, India, Pakistan and USA. All our relevant products and accessories are inspected for its quality at all stages of supply and manufacturing by a team of well-trained inspectors.
