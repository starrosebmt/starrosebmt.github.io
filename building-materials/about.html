About Us

Star Rose Group of Company

Star Rose Building Materials Trading is one of the leading and pioneer Building Materials, Safety & Industrial Equipment's suppliers in the United Arab Emirates. Our business partners and support companies are present in China, Europe, GCC, Germany, India, Pakistan and USA. All our relevant products and accessories are inspected for its quality at all stages of supply and manufacturing by a team of well-trained inspectors. We assure that we supply goods in good packing standards with On-Time delivery.

Quality is an integral part of Star Rose Building Materials Trading work style. Our aim is to achieve and sustain excellence in all the activities right from the conceptualization of the products up to finalization.

Our Vision

To provide quality products and services that exceed the expectations of our esteemed customers.

Our Mission

To build long term relationships with our clients by providing exceptional customer services by pursuing business through innovations and advanced technologies.

Company Strategy & Purpose

To be a leader in Building Materials, Construction, Safety & Industrial Equipment trading by providing enhanced services and cultivate a robust business relationship.
